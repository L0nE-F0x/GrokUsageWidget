Features

Built for daily SuperGrok use

Everything you glance at on the usage page — in a small always-ready window.

Live weekly usage

Overall SuperGrok %, category breakdown, reset countdown, and extra usage credits — refreshed on a schedule or on demand.

Sleek pill mode

Collapse to a thin always-on-top pill over your terminal or IDE so you can vibe-code without losing the number.

Tray tooltip

Minimize to the system tray. Hover the icon to see your current % used — no need to reopen the window.

Private by design

Sign in once in a local browser profile on your machine. No cloud middleman, no multi-account SaaS — just your session.

Get started

Install in about a minute

Windows installer is ready. First live refresh may take longer while helpers download.

  1. 1

    Download the installer

    Windows: Grok-Usage-Setup.exe (~2 MB). macOS: GitHub Releases.

  2. 2

    Run setup

    Install, then launch Grok Usage from the Start Menu.

  3. 3

    Connect your account

    Click Connect my Grok account, sign in when the browser opens, and wait for your numbers.

  4. 4

    Use it daily

    Pin on top, hide to tray, or switch to sleek mode while you work.
